  1. To check your current reservation status of your wait list or RAC (Reservation Against Cancellation) tickets, click on PNR Status.
  2. Live train running information can be checked by clicking on Train Running Status link.
  3. To check bogie or coach position in a train, click on Train Coach Position.
  4. To get detailed time table of various Indian Railway trains, click on Train Schedule.
  5. Railway station codes can be found by visiting Station Codes link.
  6. Visit Indian Railway link Trains Between Stations to find list of trains between source and destination stations.
  7. To find train name and train number, just go to landing page of Train Name & Number.
  8. Click on Train Ticket Booking - IRCTC if you want to book train tickets.
  9. Alternatively you can also visit http://enquiry.indianrail.gov.in which is official Indian Railway enquiry portal developed by National Train Enquiry System (NTES).

Welcome to the train enquiry portal of Trainspnrstatus. It is a one-stop online website for various types of railway enquiry. One can check PNR status, train running status and train schedule. Finding station code was never easy before, but Trainspnrstatus has made it simple and easy. Getting the list of trains between stations is much easy now. All you have to do is to provide your starting station and ending station.If you are considering other ways to find train information, then you have an option to go to the official IRCTC train enquiry portal. You can also use the official National Train Enquiry System (NTES) developed by Indian Railways. Some people find it difficult to use online Indian Railway enquiry due to less technical knowledge. For such people, Indian Railways has given a twenty-four-hour railway enquiry number 139. One can call this number and get help from a railway customer care executive immediately. It is important to note that your telecom provider may charge you for this call.If you are looking to book a train ticket, you should click the IRCTC train ticket booking link above. It is the only website allowed to book train tickets online by Indian Railways. Though some other websites too offer such services, they are the third party agent of IRCTC. Using third-party agent platforms may increase your ticket price due to additional charges. If you have noticed, IRCTC also includes a very smooth enquiry system for express trains.
